How To Add Your Product

Aap apni products sirf `script.js` me add kar sakte ho.

Step 1

`script.js` ke start me `const products = [...]` wali list open karein.

Step 2

Har product ka `id`, `name`, `category`, `price`, `tag`, aur `image` replace ya add karein.

Step 3

Image ke liye online image URL use karein. Baad me local image path bhi laga sakte hain.

Sample Product Format

{
  id: 7,
  name: "Classic Black Kurta",
  category: "Men",
  price: 49,
  tag: "New",
  image: "https://your-image-link.jpg"
}
